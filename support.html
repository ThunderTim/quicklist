Frequently Asked Questions

How does QuickList work?

Simply take photos of your items, and our AI assistant will help create professional listings with detailed descriptions and suggested pricing.

Which marketplaces are supported?

Currently, we support copy and pasting to any marketplace although our listing format is based of of Facebook Marketplace. We're constantly working to add more platforms.
