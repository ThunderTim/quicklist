Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Storage

QuickList is designed with privacy in mind. All listing data, including photos and generated descriptions, are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage statistics.

Image Processing

Our app utilizes advanced AI technology, including OpenAI's API, to analyze images and generate listing content. During this process, image data is temporarily processed using secure protocols. This processing is governed by OpenAI's privacy policy and security practices. You can learn more at OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All data processing is done securely using HTTPS encryption. Your listings and photos remain private on your device and are not stored on our servers.

Future Updates

If we add features that collect usage statistics or other data in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify users before implementing such changes.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@quicklistassist.com
