Privacy Policy

Information Collection and Storage

QuickList is designed with privacy in mind. All listing data, including photos and generated descriptions, are stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage statistics.

Image Processing and AI Technology

Our app utilizes advanced AI technology, including OpenAI's API, to analyze images and generate listing content. During this process:

  • Images are temporarily processed through OpenAI's API using secure encryption
  • Images are not permanently stored on OpenAI's servers
  • Processing is strictly limited to generating descriptions for your listings
  • No personal information is collected or stored during this process

This processing is governed by OpenAI's privacy policy and security practices. You can learn more at OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All data processing is done securely using HTTPS encryption. Your listings and photos remain private on your device and are not stored on our servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any temporary data processing.

Your Data Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access your data (all data is stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by deleting the app from your device
  • Opt-out of AI processing features by not using the description generation feature
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns or questions

Terms of Use

QuickList operates under Apple's standard End User License Agreement (EULA). You can review the full EULA at Apple's Standard EULA.

Future Updates

If we add features that collect usage statistics or other data in the future, we will update this privacy policy and notify users before implementing such changes. Any such changes will be clearly communicated through app updates.

Contact Us
